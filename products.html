HYGIENE

Our soaps are made of all natural plant oils.

Toothbrushes are made of plant oils.

All toothpastes are made from coconut oil extract.

Towels are made by using fresh cotton and dying it using plant dye.

ORGANIC FOOD

Veggies come in bunches of 5 (carrots, tomatoes, spinach, cucumbers, and bell peppers) and fruits in 4 (apples, oranges, bananas, and lemons).

Whole grain and other yummy cereals can be donated.

Dairy products are stored in our shelters' fridges so they don't rot. The homeless have the ability to claim some of them for their own use.

CLOTHES

Sweatshirts come in all colors and sizes. Select your preference.

Sweatpants come in only 2 colors, gray and black, but all sizes are available.

Shoes are available only in black, but all sizes are available.

Beanies come in all colors.

MISCELLANEOUS

Tents can also be donated to provide temporary shelter.

Pads and other menstrual products may be donated for women in need.

Cozy blankets are also donatable.

First aid kits are very useful in case of any injury.
