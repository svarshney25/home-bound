HOME BOUND

At Home Bound, we aspire to provide homeless people with access to free food and resources. Our goal is to make sure no community member is left behind!

How It Works

Anyone can help the homeless! In exchange for community service hours, you can choose products that we provide to the homeless for free. Each hour is equal to one product of your choice donated. You must be signed in to submit your volunteer hours and donate products. The flyer below can be distributed among your peers to help the homeless.
